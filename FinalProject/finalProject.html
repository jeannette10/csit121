Lessons Learned

Completing this website was both a challenging and rewarding experience. One of the biggest hurdles I faced was ensuring a consistent design across all pages while keeping the navigation intuitive. Initially, I struggled with CSS positioning and making elements responsive, but after multiple iterations and testing, I found effective solutions using flexbox and media queries. Another key learning moment was troubleshooting broken links. I learned the importance of relative vs. absolute paths when linking pages internally.

Through debugging with browser developer tools, I adjusted styles and ensured a more uniform experience. Mobile testing also highlighted the necessity of touch-friendly buttons and appropriate font sizes, making me more mindful of user accessibility across devices.

This course has truly transformed how I approach web development. Before, I primarily focused on writing functional code, but now I understand the importance of structure, styling, and accessibility. Learning about responsive design, semantic HTML, and CSS techniques has deepened my understanding of how modern websites are built. Going forward, I plan to continue refining my skills, especially in JavaScript, to enhance user interactivity.
